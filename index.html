The Story Behind the Game

This unique game was born from the imagination of an 8-year-old boy who had a vision of an epic battle between a spinning top and fearsome dragons. What makes this game special is that it was entirely created using AI tools, showing how artificial intelligence can help bring a child's creativity to life.

The young creator started with a simple sketch on paper, drawing his vision of the battle scene. From there, we embarked on an exciting journey of turning this sketch into a fully playable game using various AI tools.

AI-Powered Development

This game was built entirely using Cursor, an AI-powered development environment. Through multiple iterations with AI, we added exciting features like:

  • Multiple levels with increasingly challenging dragons
  • Dynamic special effects for explosions and impacts
  • Intelligent dragon behaviors and attack patterns
  • Health and ammo management systems
